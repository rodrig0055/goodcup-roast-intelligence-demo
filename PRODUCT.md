# Product

## Register

product

## Users

GoodCup's head roaster, R&D team, and cuppers. They work between the cupping lab and roast floor and need answers without becoming statisticians or software operators.

## Product Purpose

Turn GoodCup's own green, roast, and cupping records into an honest operational memory. The product should help the team identify associations worth testing, inspect roast curves, spot cupper drift, and understand whether the dataset is mature enough for recommendation work.

## Brand Personality

Grounded, exact, quietly optimistic. The product should feel like a trusted scientific instrument shaped by people who care about coffee, not a generic business-intelligence suite.

## Anti-references

Avoid dark control-room dashboards, rustic coffee-shop styling, beige SaaS card grids, decorative bean imagery, and AI interfaces that overstate certainty.

## Design Principles

1. Put the decision before the chart.
2. Make uncertainty visible at the point of interpretation.
3. Use GoodCup's identity with restraint.
4. Keep expert workflows familiar and fast.
5. Label demo data and future capabilities honestly.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast, keyboard-visible controls, color-independent status cues, readable chart labels, and reduced-motion support.
